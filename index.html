About Us

Whether you’re looking for a quiet spot to work or a social hub to meet new people, we cater for all guests and all group sizes. All our baristas are extensivley trained with all coffee roasts and brew types. From drip coffee to Machiatos, or anything inbetween.

Our Roast Types

At Union, we offer a range of roast types from light roasts with sweeter milder notes, to our signature dark roasts, with earthy rich tones.

Explore our wide range of roasts, rich darks and mild lights alike.
